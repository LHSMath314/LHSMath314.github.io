Core & Advanced Topics

These topics range from high school essentials to fascinating ideas found in advanced mathematics. Some may be familiar, others may open completely new worlds.

Trigonometry

Exploring the relationships between angles and sides of triangles, and their applications.

Pre-Calculus

Preparing for calculus through functions, limits, and algebraic reasoning.

Calculus

The mathematics of change — derivatives, integrals, and the beauty of continuous motion.

Euler’s Identity

The elegant equation that connects five of math’s most important constants.

Complex Analysis

Diving into the mysterious and beautiful world of complex numbers and functions.

Hypercomplex Numbers

Going beyond the complex plane — quaternions, octonions, and higher dimensions.

Number Theory

The study of integers, primes, and the patterns that hide within them.

Probability & Statistics

Understanding data, randomness, and how math helps make sense of uncertainty.

Topology

Exploring shapes, spaces, and surfaces that defy traditional geometry.
